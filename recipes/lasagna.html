World's Best Lasagna



Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat sauce (ragù), creamy béchamel sauce, and cheese. It's a delicious and comforting meal that's perfect for gatherings or family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (oven-ready or pre-boiled)
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage (you can also use a combination of both)
  • Onion, garlic, and other vegetables (optional)
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Béchamel sauce (white sauce made with butter, flour, and milk)
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella and parmesan cheese
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian herbs (such as basil, oregano, and thyme)

Ingredients

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on the lasagna noodle package.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. If you're using vegetables, add them to the skillet and sauté until softened.
  3. Pour in the tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes and season with salt, pepper, and Italian herbs. Let the sauce simmer for about 15-20 minutes.
  4. In a separate saucepan, prepare the béchamel sauce by melting butter, stirring in flour, and slowly whisking in milk until it thickens.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce at the bottom. Add a layer of lasagna noodles on top, followed by a layer of ricotta cheese and béchamel sauce. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella and parmesan cheese over the sauce.
  6. Repeat the layers until you run out of ingredients, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and shredded cheese on top.
  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  8. Let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before serving to allow it to set.
