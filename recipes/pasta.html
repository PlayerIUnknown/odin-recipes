World's Best Pasta



Description

Pasta is a versatile Italian staple that can be paired with various sauces and toppings. Here, we'll prepare a simple spaghetti dish with a classic tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti or your favorite pasta shape
  • Tomato sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • Garlic and onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian herbs (such as basil and oregano)
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Recipe

  1. Cook the pasta according to the instructions on the package until it's al dente (firm to the bite). Drain the pasta and set it aside, reserving a little pasta water.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and diced onion and saut√© until they become fragrant and translucent.
  3. Pour in the tomato sauce and season with salt, pepper, and Italian herbs. Let the sauce simmer for a few minutes to develop the flavors.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss it with the sauce until the pasta is evenly coated. If the sauce seems too thick, add a splash of the reserved pasta water to loosen it up.
  5. Serve the pasta hot, garnished with grated parmesan cheese if desired.
